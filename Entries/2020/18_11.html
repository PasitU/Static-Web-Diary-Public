~ บุรุษไม่เอ่ยนามชื่อขึ้นต้นด้วย B

Hoo boy, how should I start, that was one hell of a cooking class. I and Ten was left in charge of heating up the oil. We brought the pot, put it over the flame and wait. That's when we noticed that the bottom of the pot wasn't dry, in fact there's still several drops of water rolling around. We were told it would dry up before we know so we didn't pay it any mind. Minutes have passed and it was still there, albeit way smaller than before. The order came, "sure, pour the oil in" we were told. We didn't hesitate even a moment.

Artistic depiction of the dreadful event

Here we go, what followed was the worst sound possible to emanate. First it was but a little bit of crackling then turned into a full blown fire blast emerging from the pot just like a monster, a beast coming out from a magic cauldron. We were frozen stiff, unable to move. Terrified of the nefarious untamed blaze engulfing the air above the metal vessel right in front of use. Luckily, the teacher rushed in and extinguished the flame before anything worse happened. We were scolded but the teacher didn't punish us since it was her order after all. We were given a time-out to get some fresh air and that's about it.

10/10 would do it again.
