[24/01/20] Decoy Crafting

The decoy

"นี่มันไม่ควรเป็นไปได้!!!"

~ คนปิเปตต์หมายเลข 1(Author)

      We built a decoy trap student, carefully engineered by Pasit, KJ, PP, Yong, Ten, Ball. The plan was simple, to trick and divert attention from our English teacher. The ragdoll was built from a bagpack full of books, a hoodie jacket, a basketball, a piece of paper and a straw hat. The components were put together into the following amalgamation.

The head was made using basketball, a face was drawn on a piece of paper. A school bag was used as the body. Finally when we put the plan into motion the teacher saw right through us and forced us to dismantle him in to scraps, he was then turned in glue and dogfood.
