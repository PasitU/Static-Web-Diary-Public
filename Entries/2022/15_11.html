So far so good?

     It's been a good while since I left high school and embarked on a new journey at KMUTT. I still am unsure of my own feeling, the loneliness the fear of strangers. There are a lot of things I need to get used to but it'll have to wait since I rarely have any time to explore the campus. My scores are pretty good that's no surprise to me, but it's a lot harder to plan my move in long term unlike the high school life. The professor are Mostly nice and reasonable. But it's been pretty difficult for me to open up to strangers let alone befriend them, it's probably my fault for being so close minded but I still can't let go of my past. Those days were but halcyon, the good old days, that cannot be rewinded. I know I should start open the new door in front of me and walk onward, never to give in to the call of yesterday, the memory that made those day sublime has long passed. Many friends that were considered as comrades, rivals, buddies, or even all of those combined have already move onward with a path, a goal, a hope, a dream a new battle of their own. They should be fine, I should be fine. I thank you all for your comradery and support during the trying time.

10/10 would do all over it again with you guys.
