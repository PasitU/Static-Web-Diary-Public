[26/11/20] Reward of labor

Fruit of labor

"จงรักษาความดีไว้เหมือนเกลือรักษาความเค็ม"

~ ครูสอนสังคม

     I thought it was impossible, but it's happening all because of my hard work. The diploma reward only to be bestowned upon the brightest students one who need to receive three certificate awards consecutively. After 3 grueling years of studying and earning teacher trusts, I am finally worthy of such honor. At first, I thought I would be but a simple no name, no face, no fame student, I can finally leave those day at ease knowing I had build myself a legacy. Now, I have realized to push myself to new even greater heights is truly sublime. Supremacy and Superiority are things that I will strive for from now on.
